Things to Consider

Remember, you can access a range of risk management tools built to help you get the most out of your trades.

  • Stop-loss order – closes your trade when it falls to a predetermined level to protect your margin.

  • AvaProtect – A unique tool which you can set per trade to protect your position from moving against you for a set duration.
